You Buy - We Ship

Due to Logistical challenges, most online stores are unable to ship directly from the USA to Kenya.

Titan has come up with solutions to this by providing the following services:

. Client purchases the items online and gets them delivered to our US address. Once we receive the items, we repackage them and consolidate them to ensure the customer gets value for their money when it comes to shipping.

. Clients pays for the items at our Kenya Office, who communicates the order to pur US office for purchase. The items are received, consolidated and repackaged. They get billed for shipping and dispatched to our Kenyan office.

The client is updated throughout the process.

Flow of items

Item Purchased (By Client or US Office)
Item Received by our US Office
Item Consolidated by our US Office
Item Invoiced and Shipped from the US to Kenya
Item Received by Kenya Office
Item Owner Notified
Client Pays the Shipping Charges and Pick the Item

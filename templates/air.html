Shipments every Week from Seattle

Air shipment is done every week. Packages are received, repackaged, and dispatched on Tuesdays. For most items, charges are based on weight and is done by taking the higher between volumetric weight and actual weight. Electronics like laptops and phones are charged per device. There is a minimum charge of $50 per shipment. Special rate applies for large consignments and shipments above 100 kgs.

INSURANCE - Air shipment is required to have insurance coverage at the rate of 2% for non-electronic items value and 5% for electronics..

PRICING - Use our Cost Calculator to know the shipping price.
